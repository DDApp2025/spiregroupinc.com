How Ariel works

Four things Ariel does on every call.

No busy signal. No voicemail. No web form that no one reads. Ariel picks up, understands, and moves the caller forward, then tells you exactly what happened.

01 / Answers
She picks up in two rings
Every inbound call, 24 hours a day, answered within two rings in a natural voice. Email and inbox messages too. Callers never hit voicemail, never wait on hold, and never reach a dead line after hours.
02 / Educates
She answers from content you approved
Ariel explains your services, hours, policies, and the prices you set, using only answers you approved. She does not improvise pricing or make promises you did not authorize. When a caller needs a person, she escalates.
03 / Books
She books while the caller is on the line
Ariel checks your live availability and books the caller straight into your open schedule before they hang up, then sends the confirmation. The appointment is on your calendar while the interest is still hot.
04 / Reports
She tells you what every call was worth
After each call, Ariel logs who called, what they wanted, what she did, and the value of the booking. You see every recovered call in one place, so the phone stops being a black box.

Aesthetic and medical practices

Built in a real clinical environment, not a lab.

Spire Group built Ariel for Aesthetics To Go, our own fully autonomous operating system. That means she was engineered from the start for the demands of medical and aesthetic front desks, where a wrong answer is not just a bad experience.

Ariel is HIPAA-capable, handles consent the way a clinical workflow requires, escalates to your team the moment a call should reach a human, and discloses that she is an AI assistant on every call. She is designed to protect the practice and the patient, not to pretend to be something she is not.

Questions

Ariel, answered.

What is Ariel?
Ariel is an AI receptionist built and operated by Spire Group Inc. She answers your inbound phone calls and email within two rings, 24 hours a day, responds using only the answers you approve, and books callers straight into your open schedule.
How does Ariel know what to say?
Ariel answers only from content you approve: your services, hours, policies, and the prices you set. She does not improvise pricing or make promises you did not authorize. If a caller needs a human, Ariel escalates to your team.
Can Ariel book appointments?
Yes. Ariel checks your live availability and books the caller directly into your schedule while they are still on the line, then sends a confirmation. She works your phones and your inbox around the clock.
Is Ariel suitable for medical and aesthetic practices?
Yes. Spire Group built Ariel for Aesthetics To Go, so she is engineered to be HIPAA-capable, with consent handling, human escalation, and an AI disclosure on every call. Sensitive workflows route to your team when they should.
