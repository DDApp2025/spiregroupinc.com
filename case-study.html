The Problem

What happens when the clinic comes to you?

Aesthetic treatment — Botox, dermal fillers, hair restoration — has historically required a physical clinic or med spa. Patients drive to appointments. Providers work fixed locations. The infrastructure assumption is a building.

Aesthetics To Go was founded on a different premise: vetted, credentialed aesthetic providers traveling directly to patients — at home, at the office, wherever they prefer. No waiting rooms. No clinic overhead. Clinical-grade treatment delivered on the patient's terms.

The technical challenge was building a custom healthcare platform — a cloud-native SaaS operating system — that makes this safe, scalable, and regulatorily defensible, with no prior model to follow. Every process that a physical clinic handles through physical infrastructure had to be reimagined as software: credentialing, consent, scheduling, route optimization, inventory, clinical documentation, and compliance.

The Platform

Eight systems. One operating layer.

The ATG platform is not a scheduling app with some clinical features added. It's a full clinical operating system with eight integrated subsystems — each purpose-built, each designed to work together as a single operating layer for mobile aesthetic delivery.
