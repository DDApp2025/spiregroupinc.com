Three principles that drive every stack decision

01
Production-proven, not evaluated
Every tool in this stack has run in a live clinical platform under real-world conditions. We don't recommend technology we haven't operated ourselves at production scale.
02
Compliance-first architecture
HIPAA-awareness isn't a layer you add. It's a constraint that shapes technology selection from the beginning. Every infrastructure choice is made with data sensitivity, auditability, and access control in view.
03
Auditability over cleverness
In clinical systems, the ability to trace what happened — and why — matters more than technical elegance. We choose boring, auditable patterns over clever, opaque ones. Every agent writes to a log. Every decision leaves a record.
