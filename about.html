About Spire Group Inc.

Built by engineers and
industry-leading aesthetic professionals —
combining deep clinical knowledge
with operational excellence.

Spire Group Inc. is a technology development company based in Las Vegas, Nevada. We specialize in AI-powered clinical and healthcare SaaS platforms — complex, multi-stakeholder systems that require deep technical architecture, regulatory awareness, and real operational understanding.

We don't consult on what to build. We build it — and we operate it. Aesthetics To Go is the evidence: a two-year investment in clinical AI technology that is now a live, protected, continuously improving platform.

A development firm that carries the weight of its own work

Most software development firms hand off a deliverable and move on. Their accountability ends at launch. Ours doesn't — because we operate our own platform in the same environment we build for clients.

When we make an architectural decision in the ATG platform, we live with the consequences. When we design a HIPAA-aware data model, we're the ones responsible if it's wrong. When we build a credentialing workflow, real providers and patients depend on it working correctly.

That operational accountability shapes how we approach every engagement. We build for the long run, because we run our own platform and plan to run it for the long term.

Infotech Inc. operates through two entities: Infotech Inc. handles all development, IP, and technology work; Spire Group Inc. manages app store developer accounts. Both are controlled by the same founding engineer who built ATG from the ground up.
