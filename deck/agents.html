Agent System Architecture

Seven specialized agents handle acquisition, engagement, and clinical operations autonomously, across both the provider and patient sides of the platform. Built as a core layer of our cloud-native SaaS architecture, the agent system is designed for reliability and auditability, with human oversight built into the workflow.

Agent Job
Scout Discover and verify providers, patients, and prospective customers across multiple data sources integrated into the SaaS platform
Intel Research and profile each prospect: specialties, credentials, licensing status, needs, and optimal outreach strategy
Forge Generate personalized outreach, patient communications, marketing assets, and clinical content
Dispatch Deliver messages across all channels with rate limiting, timing optimization, and sequencing
Sentinel Monitor inbound responses, appointment activity, and clinical workflow events; classify, prioritize, and route
Closer Handle warm leads, manage follow-ups, book appointments, and support patient conversion
Command Orchestrate all agents within the SaaS platform, aggregate operational data, generate reports, and surface decisions for review

Architecture Pattern

Agents communicate through the SaaS platform's shared data infrastructure, not direct agent-to-agent messaging, keeping the system auditable, debuggable, and reliable. API development follows a service-oriented pattern, and Command surfaces results to a daily dashboard requiring approximately 30–60 minutes of human review per day.
