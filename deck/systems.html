Cloud-Native SaaS Platform — Aesthetics To Go

01 Clinical Scheduling & Route Optimization

Manages provider availability, patient booking, and geographic route planning for mobile clinical services within the multi-tenant SaaS architecture. Integrates real-time scheduling with drive-time calculations to maximize provider utilization. Supports recurring appointments, waitlists, and automated rescheduling.

02 Provider Credentialing & Compliance

Automates NPI verification, state license validation, and credential tracking for all providers on the platform. Maintains expiration alerts and renewal workflows to ensure continuous compliance. Integrates with state licensing boards and federal registries for real-time verification.

03 Patient Intake & Consent Management

Digital intake forms, medical history collection, and electronic consent capture — all HIPAA-compliant. Supports procedure-specific consent workflows with provider countersignature. Records are versioned and immutable for legal and regulatory auditability.

04 EHR & SOAP Notes Documentation

A custom-built SaaS solution for structured clinical documentation following the SOAP format — Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan. AI-assisted note generation reduces provider documentation burden while maintaining clinical accuracy. Integrates with the consent and scheduling systems for complete encounter records.

05 AI Procedure Simulation Engine

Computer vision pipeline that processes patient images to generate realistic treatment simulations, accessible through both our cross-platform mobile app development for iOS and Android and the provider web portal. Patients can preview expected outcomes before committing to procedures, improving conversion and satisfaction. Uses generative image AI calibrated against real clinical outcomes data.

06 Medical AI Assistant & LLM Integration

Powered by a proprietary healthcare LLM fine-tuned on aesthetic medicine datasets, the medical assistant supports both providers and patients throughout the clinical workflow. Delivers treatment recommendations, clinical decision support, provider education, and patient-facing Q&A with domain-specific accuracy that generic models cannot match. All outputs are flagged for provider review — the AI advises, clinicians decide.

07 Inventory & Supply Chain Management

Tracks product inventory, expiration dates, lot numbers, and reorder thresholds across all service locations. Integrates with scheduling to forecast supply needs based on upcoming appointments and procedure types. Supports multi-location inventory transfers and vendor purchase order automation.

08 7-Agent Autonomous Marketing System

The full agent pipeline — Scout, Intel, Forge, Dispatch, Sentinel, Closer, and Command — operating autonomously within the SaaS platform to acquire providers and patients. Handles prospecting, outreach, follow-up, and conversion with human oversight via the Command dashboard. Detailed in the Agent System Architecture section of this deck.

Clinical Operations Playbook

A comprehensive operational framework including complete SOPs for every clinical workflow, treatment protocols aligned with manufacturer guidelines and medical board standards, staff training guides, and an AI-powered tutoring system for onboarding new providers and clinical staff.

Clinical Image & Treatment Mapping

A structured data layer linking patient images to injection sites, product quantities, dilution ratios, and application methods. This mapping powers the AI simulation engine, supports clinical documentation, and enables outcome tracking across treatments and providers.
