Development Phases

Phase 1

Foundation

SaaS platform architecture and cross-platform mobile app development for iOS and Android using React Native, .NET/C# backend API, AWS infrastructure provisioning, core scheduling and booking engine, and provider profile management. The base layer everything else builds on.

Phase 2

Intelligence

Custom software development of the procedure simulation engine using computer vision and generative AI, treatment recommendation system, Claude API medical AI assistant integration, and clinical protocol design developed with board-certified medical oversight.

Phase 3

Compliance

NPI verification and license validation pipelines, HIPAA-aware data architecture across the entire multi-tenant SaaS platform, Business Associate Agreements with all vendors and partners, digital consent management workflows, and corporate IP framework establishment.

Phase 4

IP Protection

Provisional patent filed with the United States Patent and Trademark Office, trademark applications submitted under Classes 44 (medical services) and 9 (software), and copyright deposits filed to protect proprietary systems and content.

Phase 5

Growth

Full 7-agent marketing pipeline deployed and operational, provider database built and continuously growing, multi-channel outreach across email, social, and direct messaging, and comprehensive SEO implementation across all web properties.

Phase 6

Ongoing

Cloud-native SaaS platform live and serving patients and providers, Las Vegas launch market active and expanding, continuous iteration on AI features and clinical tools, route optimization refinement, and inventory management scaling with demand.
