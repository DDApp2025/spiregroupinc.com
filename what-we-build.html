01
Clinical Platform Architecture
Foundation Layer

Multi-sided clinical platforms are architecturally different from standard SaaS. You have patients, providers, administrators, and medical directors — each with distinct data access, workflow requirements, and compliance obligations. We've built this from the ground up and know where the complexity lives.

Multi-Stakeholder Data Model
Patient records, provider profiles, appointment data, and clinical notes in a role-based schema where access is governed by compliance requirements, not just permissions flags.
HIPAA-Aware Architecture
AWS infrastructure configured for HIPAA-eligible workloads. BAAs, encryption at rest and in transit, audit logging, and access controls built into the data layer — not bolted on afterward.
Consent & Intake Workflows
Digital informed consent, medical history intake, contraindication screening — embedded in the patient flow, not as a separate system to integrate later.
Admin & Oversight Portals
Medical director dashboards, operational admin interfaces, and compliance reporting built alongside the patient and provider experiences from day one.
Clinical Operations Playbook
A complete, structured clinical operations framework — standard operating procedures, provider protocols, compliance checklists, escalation workflows, and onboarding sequences. Not a document. A living operational layer built into the platform.
02
AI & Medical Intelligence
Intelligence Layer

Healthcare AI is not a feature you add to an existing platform — it's an architectural decision you make at the beginning. We've built treatment recommendation engines, procedure simulation tools, and clinical AI assistants that operate within defined medical protocols, not as free-form chatbots.

Procedure Simulation Engine
Real-time facial AI that shows patients their predicted treatment results before the procedure. Built using computer vision and custom ML pipelines. Deployed and operational in ATG.
Medical AI Assistant
Context-aware conversational AI embedded in the platform — trained on clinical protocols, constrained to appropriate scope, and integrated with patient and provider data.
Treatment Recommendations
Recommendation engines that factor in patient history, contraindications, provider credentials, and clinical best practices — not just popularity or availability.
LLM Integration via Claude API
Production-grade AI integration with custom system prompts, conversation history management, and clinical guardrails. Secure edge proxy for API management.
Training, Resources & AI Tutoring
Comprehensive training guides, clinical resources, and an AI-powered tutoring system built on a medical large language model. Providers and staff get on-demand education covering procedures, protocols, product knowledge, and platform operation — with domain-specific accuracy, not generic search results.
Clinical Image & Treatment Mapping
Complete management of patient images, treatment graphics, injection sites, product quantities, and application methods — mapped to each procedure and patient record. Providers document exactly what was done, where, how much, and with what product, creating a full visual and clinical treatment history.
03
Mobile-First Patient & Provider Apps
Experience Layer

Clinical mobile apps are held to a higher standard than consumer apps. They carry sensitive health information, must work reliably in field conditions, and need to earn the trust of both providers and patients. We build React Native applications that meet that standard.

React Native — iOS & Android
Single codebase deploying to both platforms. App Store and Google Play submission experience including TestFlight distribution, review guidelines navigation, and release management.
Provider Operating Interface
Scheduling, patient lookup, charting, consent capture, treatment logging, inventory check, and payment processing — all in one provider-facing interface designed for field use.
Full EHR & SOAP Notes System
A complete electronic health records system with structured SOAP note documentation — Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan — built directly into the provider workflow. Pre-populated from patient intake, linked to treatment images and product records, and stored with full compliance audit trails. Not a third-party EHR integration. A purpose-built clinical documentation system designed for mobile aesthetic delivery.
Patient-Facing Experience
Booking, provider selection, pre-treatment intake, AI simulation preview, appointment reminders, and post-treatment follow-up — designed for trust, clarity, and conversion.
Payments & Stripe Integration
Full Stripe integration for patient payments, provider payouts, and platform fee processing. Compliant card handling with no sensitive data touching our servers.
04
Scheduling & Route Optimization
Operations Layer

For mobile clinical delivery — providers traveling to patients — scheduling is not a calendar problem. It's a logistics problem. We've built smart scheduling engines that optimize provider routes in real time, balance appointment density against travel time, and tie inventory to scheduled procedures.

Real-Time Route Optimization
Dynamic routing that maximizes provider daily capacity by minimizing drive time between appointments — accounting for location, duration, and supply requirements.
Territory & Coverage Planning
Provider territory assignment, coverage gap analysis, and demand heatmapping to guide expansion decisions and provider recruitment targeting.
Procedure-Linked Inventory
The scheduling engine knows what supplies each appointment requires. Providers are flagged before departure if inventory is insufficient — eliminating wasted appointments.
Availability & Booking Logic
Provider availability management, patient-facing booking slots, buffer time rules, and appointment confirmation workflows — all configurable per provider and procedure type.
05
Provider Credentialing & Compliance
Compliance Layer

Putting credentialed clinical providers in front of patients requires a credentialing system that is thorough, auditable, and embedded in the platform — not a spreadsheet or a manual checklist. We've built this end-to-end for aesthetics; the architecture applies across clinical specialties.

NPI Verification
Real-time NPI lookup against the NPPES registry. Automated verification of provider type, taxonomy, and license status before onboarding is approved.
License Validation Workflows
State license verification, expiration tracking, and renewal reminders — integrated into the provider profile so compliance status is always current and visible to administrators.
Independent Contractor Management
Agreement execution, version control, and signature tracking for provider independent contractor agreements — with medical director oversight documentation built in.
HIPAA BAAs & Consent Management
Business Associate Agreements executed with all relevant parties. Patient consent forms versioned, signed digitally, and stored with full audit trail in the platform data layer.

Prospecting & Enrichment Agents

Personalized Outreach Generation
AI-powered message generation that creates individualized outreach for each prospect across multiple channels — at scale, without sounding templated.
Multi-Channel Dispatch
Automated sending across all channels with platform-specific rate limiting, timing rules, and sequencing — integrated with enterprise-grade automation platforms.
Response Monitoring & Lead Routing
Continuous inbound monitoring across all channels. Inbound responses automatically classified, prioritized, and routed — with high-value leads escalated for immediate action.
