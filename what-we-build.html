Clinical Platform Architecture
Foundation Layer

Multi-sided clinical platforms require distinct data access, workflows, and compliance for every stakeholder. We architect this from the ground up.

Multi-Stakeholder Data Model
Role-based data architecture governing access across patients, providers, and administrators by compliance requirements.
HIPAA-Aware Architecture
Cloud infrastructure configured for HIPAA-eligible workloads with encryption, audit logging, and access controls built in.
Consent & Intake Workflows
Digital informed consent, medical history intake, and contraindication screening embedded in the patient flow.
Admin & Oversight Portals
Medical director dashboards, admin interfaces, and compliance reporting built alongside patient and provider experiences.
Clinical Operations Playbook
Living operational framework with SOPs, provider protocols, compliance checklists, and escalation workflows built into the platform.

Procedure Simulation Engine

Medical AI Assistant

Scheduling & Route Optimization
Operations Layer

For mobile clinical delivery, scheduling is a logistics problem. We build smart scheduling engines that optimize routes, balance density, and tie inventory to procedures.

Real-Time Route Optimization
Dynamic routing that maximizes provider daily capacity by minimizing drive time between appointments.
