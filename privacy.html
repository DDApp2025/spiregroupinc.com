Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, and message content. We do not collect personal information passively beyond standard analytics data described below.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiries, evaluate potential project engagements, and improve our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This Site uses a limited number of third-party services to operate. Formspree processes contact form submissions on our behalf. Google Analytics collects anonymized site usage data to help us understand how visitors interact with the Site. GitHub Pages provides website hosting. Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

This Site uses cookies for limited purposes. Session cookies may be used to manage access to specific areas of the Site, such as the investor deck. Analytics cookies are used by Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, modification of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@spiregroupinc.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

7. HIPAA Notice

This website does not collect, store, or process protected health information (PHI). Spire Group Inc. builds clinical platforms for healthcare organizations, and those client platforms are designed and developed with HIPAA-aware architecture. However, the spiregroupinc.com website itself is a corporate informational site and does not handle any patient or clinical data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Site after any modifications constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@spiregroupinc.com.
